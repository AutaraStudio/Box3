Executive Summary

BOX3 Projects has established itself as a leading commercial fit-out specialist in London, delivering exceptional workspaces for prestigious clients including Meta, Hugo Boss, Warner Music Group, and Unison. With 180+ years of combined industry expertise and a remarkable 90% repeat business rate, your company's reputation speaks volumes about the quality of work delivered.

However, your current digital presence does not reflect the calibre of your work. This proposal outlines a comprehensive rebrand and website development project designed to position BOX3 Projects as the premium choice for commercial fit-outs in the London market.

Vision

Create a digital presence that matches the quality and attention to detail BOX3 brings to every project, establishing trust with high-value commercial clients from the first interaction.

Three Integrated Phases

  • Brand Guidelines — Foundation for all communications
  • Figma Design Mockups — High-fidelity prototypes for review
  • Website Development — Custom-built Webflow site with CMS

Target launch: Late March 2026

Scope of Work

A three-phase approach to transform your digital presence.

01

Brand Guidelines

Comprehensive brand guidelines document serving as the foundation for all communications

Logo System — Refined primary logo, secondary marks, favicon, usage rules
Colour Palette — Primary, secondary & accent colours with HEX, RGB, CMYK
Typography — Heading & body font selections with hierarchy guidelines
Photography Style — Art direction for project photography & portraits
Graphic Elements — Patterns, icons & supporting visual elements
Voice & Tone — Messaging framework & copywriting guidelines
Application Examples — Business cards, email signatures, templates
02

Figma Design Mockups

High-fidelity interactive prototypes for collaborative review before development

Homepage — Hero, services overview, featured projects, testimonials & CTAs
About Page — Company story, team profiles, values & 'Why BOX3'
Services Pages — Cat A, Cat B, Design & Build, Dilapidations, Hybrid
Portfolio — Filterable project gallery with case study template
Contact Page — Enquiry form, office location & direct contact
Responsive Designs — Desktop, tablet & mobile breakpoints
Component Library — Reusable UI elements for scalability
Interactive Prototype — Clickable prototype for review
03

Website Development

Fully custom Webflow website optimised for performance, SEO and content management

Webflow Build — Custom development with animations & interactions
CMS Integration — Full CMS setup for easy content updates
Portfolio System — Dynamic case studies with filtering
Contact Integration — Forms with email notifications
SEO Optimisation — Technical SEO, metadata & structured data
Performance — Optimised for speed and Core Web Vitals
Analytics — GA4 & conversion tracking setup
Training — CMS training session (up to 2 hours)
